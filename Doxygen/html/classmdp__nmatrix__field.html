Detailed Description

field of vectors of matrices

Example: 

///    int box[]={10,10,10};
///    mdp_lattice lattice(3,box);
///    mdp_nmatrix_field h(lattice,10,3,3);
///    mdp_site x(lattice);
///    forallsites(x)
///      for(int i=0; i<10; i++)
///        h(x,i)=lattice.random(x).SU(3);
///
