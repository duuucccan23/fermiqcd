Detailed Description

field of complex numbers or vectors of complex numbers

Example: 

///    int box[]={10,10,10};
///    mdp_lattice lattice(3,box);
///    mdp_complex_field psi(lattice,10);
///    mdp_site x(lattice);
///    forallsites(x)
///      for(int i=0; i<10; i++)
///         psi(x,i)=0.0+0.0*I;
///
